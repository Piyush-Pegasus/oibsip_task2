E-waste Facility Locator Web App

The proposed website aims to revolutionize e-waste management by providing users with a comprehensive platform to responsibly dispose of electronic devices. Users can input device details or upload images, leveraging AI to fetch product information and highlight harmful and valuable components. The system optimizes routes to nearby e-waste facilities using path optimization algorithms displayed on Google Maps or NavIC. Users can select a facility for self-delivery or request a pickup, assigning a facility employee.
